About me

Hi there!

I'm Ado Muhammad Sanni, a passionate software engineer specializing in backend development with Python & Flask framework. With a strong affinity for open-source principles, I'm dedicated to crafting robust and scalable solutions that drive innovation and empower users.

Education & Training

My educational journey began at the University of Maiduguri where I pursued a diploma course in Information and Communication Technology, laying the foundation for my passion for technology. Building upon this groundwork, I continued my pursuit of knowledge at ESTAM University, where I earned my degree in Computer Science, delving into the intricacies of software development and honing my skills in Python.
Eager to deepen my expertise, I embarked on a transformative learning experience at ALX Africa Tech School. Through rigorous training and a series of intensive tasks, I obtained a certificate in Software Engineering with a specialization in backend development. This immersive program challenged me to push the boundaries of my capabilities, equipping me with advanced techniques and insights to tackle complex backend challenges with confidence and precision.

Projects & Contributions

While I haven't yet had the opportunity to work with a company, I've been actively involved in personal projects and open-source contributions. Through these experiences, I've gained valuable hands-on experience in building backend systems, designing APIs, and optimizing database performance using Python and Flask.

Expertise

Backend Development with Python & Flask. Python is my language of choice, and I'm proficient in leveraging its capabilities to develop powerful backend solutions. With Flask, I've built RESTful APIs, handled authentication and authorization, and implemented robust error handling to ensure the reliability of web applications.

Open-Source Involvement

I'm a firm believer in the ethos of open-source software and actively contribute to various Python and Flask projects. Whether it's fixing bugs, adding new features, or improving documentation, I'm committed to giving back to the community and advancing the field of backend development.

Problem-Solving Approach

I approach challenges with a methodical and analytical mindset, always seeking elegant solutions to complex problems. With a keen eye for detail and a passion for continuous improvement, I thrive on pushing the boundaries of what's possible in backend development.

Core Values
